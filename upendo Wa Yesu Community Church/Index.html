Our Mission

At Upendo Wa Yesu Community Church, our mission is Restoring The Lost Glory Of Humanity.
We believe that through faith, love, and community, every person can rediscover their divine purpose and
live a life of fulfillment and service.

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of God's love and
grow in their spiritual journey.

Our church is built on the foundation of biblical teachings, fostering a community where
individuals and families can thrive spiritually, emotionally, and socially.

Our Special Programs

We offer various programs to support spiritual growth and community building for all ages:

Youth Ministry

Engaging activities and teachings tailored for young people to grow in faith and fellowship.
Our youth group meets every Saturday afternoon.

Women's Fellowship

A supportive community for women to connect, share, and grow spiritually together.
Meetings held on the first Tuesday of each month.

Community Outreach

Service projects and initiatives to support and uplift our local community through food drives, visitation programs and
neighborhood clean-ups.

Marriage Enrichment

Workshops and counseling to strengthen marriages and family relationships.
Special sessions available for engaged couples.

Children's Ministry

Age-appropriate biblical teaching and fun activities for children during all worship services.
Safe and nurturing environment provided.

Men's Brotherhood

Fellowship and accountability group for men seeking to grow in their faith and leadership roles within family and
community.
