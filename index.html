Our Mission

Committed to restoring humanity's divine purpose through faith, love and community

At Upendo Wa Yesu Community Church, our mission is Restoring The Lost Glory Of Humanity. We believe that through faith, love, and community, every person can rediscover their divine purpose and live a life of fulfillment and service.

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of God's love and grow in their spiritual journey.

Our church is built on the foundation of biblical teachings, fostering a community where individuals and families can thrive spiritually, emotionally, and socially.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Komamboga and beyond, where every soul finds their purpose in Christ and contributes to building God's kingdom on earth.

Our Programs

Ministries designed to support spiritual growth for all ages

Youth Ministry

Engaging activities and teachings tailored for young people to grow in faith and fellowship. Our youth group meets every Saturday afternoon with dynamic worship, relevant teachings, and community service projects.

Women's Fellowship

A supportive community for women to connect, share, and grow spiritually together. Monthly meetings feature inspiring speakers, prayer sessions, and opportunities for meaningful fellowship and mutual support.

Community Outreach

Service projects and initiatives to support and uplift our local community through food drives, visitation programs, neighborhood clean-ups, and assistance to families in need.

Marriage Enrichment

Workshops and counseling to strengthen marriages and family relationships. Special sessions available for engaged couples, with ongoing support for all stages of married life.

Children's Ministry

Age-appropriate biblical teaching and fun activities for children during all worship services. Safe and nurturing environment with trained volunteers and engaging curriculum.

Men's Brotherhood

Fellowship and accountability group for men seeking to grow in their faith and leadership roles within family and community. Monthly gatherings focus on spiritual growth and mutual encouragement.
